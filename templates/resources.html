Resume: Tips and Template

Tip #1: Tailor Your Resume
  • Customize your resume for each job you apply to by highlighting the most relevant skills and experiences
  • Use keywords from the job description
Tip #2: Keep it Concise
  • Aim for a one-page resume
  • Use bullet points to present information clearly
Tip #3: Use a Clean and Professional Design
  • Use a simple and professional design to make your resume easy to read
  • Use bold and headings to differentiate sections

Cover Letter: Tips and Template

Tip #1: Start with a Strong Introduction
  • Have a strong opening statement that emphasizes what skills you can bring to the table
  • Explain why you'd be a great fit for the job and company you are applying to
Tip #2: Convey Enthusiasm
  • Make it clear why you want the position
  • Maintain a professional and confident tone throughout your cover letter
Tip #3: Keep it Short
  • Ideally it should be one page
  • Have a strong opening, highlight your qualifications and achievements, and close with a confident call to action
